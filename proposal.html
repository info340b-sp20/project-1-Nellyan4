Quote Saver

Problem Description

Reading nowadays becomes like "eating snacks". People loves to scroll down the screen very fast and only skim through the articles or posts. For many the social media platforms, it is designed to let the users skim through the content and move towards the next. However, this is sometimes frustrating to me. Quotes motivates and inspires people to do good. While reading or browsing, it is good to note down some quotes. Sometimes when I wanted to look up for some posts, quotes, memes that I read before, I have no way to find it back. Few of the social media platforms have functions that allow people to store information. With this I mean something like an application that makes and stores beautiful quotes, so that users can read the quotes back in the future.

Proposed Application

My proposed application would be to create a website that allow users to store quote/Twitter/post/meme easily. Users include all readers and users of social media platforms such as Facebook, Twitter, Reddit. The users only have to input the author and the content of a quote or share the link of a social media post, and the website can generate a store a quote page for the users!

  • It helps prevent the "snack reading" situation.
  • People have a good feeling reading back their stored content.
